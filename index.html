About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional football player and one of the greatest footballers of all time. He was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Portugal. He is known for his speed, powerful shooting, leadership, and incredible goal-scoring ability. Currently, he plays for Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.
